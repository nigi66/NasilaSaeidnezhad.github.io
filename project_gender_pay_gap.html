Uncovering the Gender Pay Gap: Insights from Data and Interactive Dashboards

Despite decades of discussion, pay disparities between genders persist across industries and regions. In this analysis, we explore how salary varies by gender, job role, location, and experience using real-world data. Interactive dashboards allow readers to visually explore these gaps and understand underlying trends.

Key Features

  • Average salary differences between genders.
  • Pay gap percentage by job role, region, and experience level.
  • Identification of roles or regions with the largest or smallest pay gaps.
  • Outlier detection to spot unusually high or low salaries.
